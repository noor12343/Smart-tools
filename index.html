PART 1: STRATEGIC & BUSINESS ARCHITECTURE

Chapter 1. Executive Summary

Kohat Konnect is a free, community focused digital platform designed exclusively for the people of Kohat. It brings together local buying and selling, community interaction, verified local news, and essential local information into one organized platform.

Today, most local activities are scattered across multiple WhatsApp groups, Facebook pages, and informal channels. This creates confusion and lack of trust. Kohat Konnect aims to solve these problems by providing a single, structured platform.

📋 Core Project Principles:
  • User First: Every feature must solve a real problem.
  • Keep It Simple: Simple experiences create better adoption.
  • Local First: Every decision must prioritize the needs of Kohat.
  • AI With Purpose: AI will improve content quality without replacing human decisions.

Chapter 2. Business Analysis

The people of Kohat rely on multiple fragmented platforms. Information is spread across different places, making it difficult for users to quickly find trusted, organized, and up to date local content.

Platform Strengths Limitations & Vulnerabilities
WhatsApp Fast communication, large user base. No search tracking, posts disappear rapidly, zero categories.
Facebook Mass audience reach, rich media. Algorithm controlled visibility, high noise, lack local filters.

Chapter 3. Product Requirements

The first official release of Kohat Konnect will include: Home Dashboard, Marketplace Engine, Community Feed, Local News Feed, Unified Search, and Admin Portals.

Chapter 6. User Roles & Permissions

User Role Permitted Operations Constraints
Registered User Create peer listings, engage in community streams. No news publishing or admin panel access.
Super Admin Absolute ecosystem rights, news publishing. None. Requires rigorous multi-factor authentication.

Chapter 7. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Strategy

AI within Kohat Konnect functions strictly as an ambient productivity booster. Features include an AI Listing Companion (proposes titles and classes based on text) and an Ecosystem Feed Integrity Guardian to catch duplicates and abuse.

Chapter 8. Security & Moderation

Account identity vectors are bound directly to user carrier network identifiers via cryptographically signed dynamic SMS OTP matching channels. Deceptive text or duplicate postings are pulled into verification pipelines automatically.

Chapter 9. Release Framework & Metrics

The immediate deployment milestone establishes a rock-solid, production-ready MVP focusing on speed and absolute data integrity for the people of Kohat.
